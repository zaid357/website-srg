About Silk Route Global

Silk Route Global is a multidisciplinary engineering solutions provider specializing in the supply, installation, commissioning, and maintenance of essential MEP and infrastructure systems. With a commitment to quality, safety, and timely delivery, we offer complete turnkey solutions for residential, commercial, industrial, and institutional projects across India and international markets. Our expertise covers a wide spectrum of engineering services including Fire Fighting Systems, HVAC Solutions, Plumbing Systems, Water Treatment Plants, Sewage Treatment Plants, Water Bodies, Swimming Pools, and Electrical Services. We partner with top manufacturers and certified engineers to deliver reliable, efficient, and long-lasting infrastructure solutions that meet the highest industry standards. At Silkroute Global, we take pride in being a trusted partner for builders, contractors and private organizations. Ensuring that every system we deliver is built with precision, advanced technology, and compliance with national & international safety norms.
