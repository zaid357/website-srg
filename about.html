About Silk Route Global

Silk Route Global is a leading technical service provider specializing in the design, supply, installation, and maintenance of essential MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) systems. We serve residential, commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects with world-class execution standards.

With a highly skilled workforce and a commitment to international safety & quality guidelines, we focus on delivering end-to-end project solutions across Fire Fighting systems, HVAC installations, Plumbing systems, Water & Sewage Treatment Plants, Swimming Pools, Water Bodies, and Electrical works.

We believe in building long-term relationships through professionalism, transparency, reliability, and on-time delivery. At Silk Route Global, every project is handled with precision engineering, modern technology, and a customer-first mindset.
